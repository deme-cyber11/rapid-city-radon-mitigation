Your Local Radon Experts in the Black Hills

Rapid City Radon Mitigation is a licensed and insured radon mitigation company proudly serving Pennington County and the entire Black Hills region. We are committed to one thing: making your home safe from the invisible threat of radon gas.

South Dakota has the highest average indoor radon levels in the entire United States, with a statewide average exceeding 9 pCi/L — more than double the EPA action level of 4 pCi/L. Rapid City and Pennington County are no exception. The geological makeup of the Black Hills, rich in uranium-bearing granite, makes radon a serious concern for every homeowner in our region.

Radon is the second leading cause of lung cancer in the United States. It is a colorless, odorless radioactive gas that seeps into homes through cracks in foundations, gaps around pipes, and sump pits. Without proper testing and mitigation, elevated radon levels put your family at risk every single day.

Our team brings hands-on expertise and a deep understanding of local building conditions. Every mitigation system we design is custom-fitted to your home's foundation type and radon concentration level. We use proven sub-slab depressurization technology to reduce radon levels to well below the EPA action level — and we guarantee our results.

Whether you need a professional radon test, a full mitigation system installation, or a fan replacement for an existing system, we are here to help. We offer free estimates, transparent pricing, and same-day service availability for urgent situations.
