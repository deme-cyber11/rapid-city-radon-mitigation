Services

Rapid City Radon Mitigation provides professional radon testing, radon mitigation system installation, sub-slab depressurization, radon fan replacement, and post-mitigation testing services to residential and commercial properties in Rapid City, South Dakota and the surrounding Black Hills region.

All services are performed by licensed and insured professionals in accordance with EPA guidelines and applicable South Dakota regulations. The specific scope, timeline, and cost of services will be outlined in a written estimate or service agreement provided prior to the commencement of work.

Post-mitigation testing is provided to verify system performance at the time of installation.
